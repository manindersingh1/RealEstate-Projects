About Samra

Samra Zafar is an International Speaker, Human Rights Activist, Scholar, Author and Social Entrepreneur. She is one of 8 highly distinguished alumni to serve as a Governor for the University of Toronto, and the youngest person to receive that honour. Samra will be speaking at TEDx Mississauga 2017 about domestic abuse, women’s rights to respect, and what individuals and communities can do to make a difference.
